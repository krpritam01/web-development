10. What are global attributes? List any 5 global attributes ?

Ans: Global attributes in HTML are attributes that can be used on any HTML element, regardless of its type. These attributes provide common functionality and behavior to elements throughout the HTML document. Here are five commonly used global attributes.

Some of the global attributes are:
    1. Class.
    2. id.
    3. title.
    4. style.
    5. tabIndex.
